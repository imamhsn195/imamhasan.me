01 — About

A backend-leaning engineer who ships end-to-end.

I'm Imam Hasan — a software engineer based in Dubai, building production systems since 2018 with a deep focus on Laravel and Spring Boot for high-throughput JVM work. Currently at X Hub Technologies LLC, leading engineering on the Nolo platform: a 13-store retail e-commerce portfolio, a multi-tenant warehouse fulfilment hub, and the companion Nolo iOS POS app.

My work tends to live where business logic gets real: payment gateways with idempotent webhooks, conversion tracking that survives ad-blockers, warehouse inventory with bin-level truth, audit-grade order workflows, and OTP/TOTP auth flows that don't get owned. I write the kind of services that have to be right because money moves through them.

Outside the day job I publish open-source Laravel packages (laravel-system-logs, laravel-Interactive-map, laravel-theme-manager), and maintain a broad set of side projects on GitHub.

Specialised in

  • Payment integrations — N-Genius, Tamara, Tabby, X-Terms, Stripe
  • Conversion tracking — Meta CAPI, TikTok Events, Stape sGTM
  • Warehouse & fulfilment systems — bin-level inventory, OrderWorkflow state machines
  • Auth hardening — TOTP 2FA, OTP rate-limiting, RBAC with Spatie
  • Push notifications — VAPID web push, APNs, FCM, service workers
  • Mobile APIs — Sanctum bearer tokens, HMAC-signed metrics endpoints
  • ERP integrations — Odoo XML-RPC, CHUB warehouse sync
  • Couriers — Quiqup, SMSA, Halan, Topex, GetGive, DHL
